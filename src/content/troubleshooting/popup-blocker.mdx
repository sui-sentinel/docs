# Fix Popup Blocker Issues

If you're getting an error like "Failed to open popup. It may have been blocked by the browser" when trying to mint NFTs, your browser is blocking the Obsidion wallet popup. Here's how to fix it:

<figure>
  <>![Allow Popup](/img/docs/allow_popup.png)</>
  <figcaption>Allow popup example in brave browser</figcaption>
</figure>

## Quick Fix Links

**For Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Brave, Edge), paste these URLs directly in your address bar:**

- **Brave**: `brave://settings/content/popups`
- **Chrome**: `chrome://settings/content/popups`
- **Edge**: `edge://settings/content/popups`

Then add these sites to the "Allowed to send pop-ups and use redirects" section:

- `https://app.obsidion.xyz` _(required - this is the wallet)_
- `https://app.ravenhouse.xyz` _(recommended - this is our app)_

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### ✅ Google Chrome

1. Open Chrome
2. Click the 3-dot menu (top-right corner) → **Settings**
3. In the left sidebar, click **Privacy and security**
4. Click **Site settings**
5. Scroll down and click **Pop-ups and redirects**
6. Under "Customized behaviors", click **Add** next to "Allowed to send pop-ups and use redirects"
7. Add these sites one by one:
   - `https://app.obsidion.xyz`
   - `https://app.ravenhouse.xyz`

### ✅ Brave Browser

1. Open Brave
2. Click the 3-line menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Go to **Privacy and security** → **Site and Shields Settings**
4. Click **Pop-ups and redirects**
5. Click **Add** under "Allowed to send pop-ups and use redirects"
6. Add these sites:
   - `https://app.obsidion.xyz`
   - `https://app.ravenhouse.xyz`

### ✅ Mozilla Firefox

1. Open Firefox
2. Click the 3-line menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Click **Privacy & Security** from the sidebar
4. Scroll to **Permissions** → look for **Block pop-up windows**
5. Click **Exceptions...** and add:
   - `https://app.obsidion.xyz`
   - `https://app.ravenhouse.xyz`

### ✅ Microsoft Edge

1. Open Edge
2. Click the 3-dot menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Navigate to **Cookies and site permissions**
4. Scroll down and click **Pop-ups and redirects**
5. Click **Add** under **Allow** section and add:
   - `https://app.obsidion.xyz`
   - `https://app.ravenhouse.xyz`

### ✅ Safari (macOS)

1. Open Safari
2. Go to top menu → **Safari** > **Settings** (or Preferences)
3. Go to the **Websites** tab
4. Select **Pop-up Windows** in the sidebar
5. For both sites, choose **Allow** from the dropdown:
   - `app.obsidion.xyz`
   - `app.ravenhouse.xyz`

## Why These Sites?

- **`app.obsidion.xyz`** - The Obsidion wallet that opens in a popup when you mint
- **`app.ravenhouse.xyz`** - Our NFT marketplace that initiates the wallet popup

## Still Having Issues?

If you're still experiencing problems after following these steps:

1. **Refresh the page** after changing settings
2. **Clear your browser cache** and try again
3. **Temporarily disable ad blockers** (they might interfere)
4. **Try a different browser** to isolate the issue
