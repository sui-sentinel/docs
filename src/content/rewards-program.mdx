# Raven Rewards Program

## Overview

**Raven Rewards Program** incentivizes user engagement and builds a loyal community through **Raven Points (RP)** earned by completing **Missions**. These points unlock exclusive NFTs, discounts, governance rights, and future token allocations.  
**Target**: NFT creators, traders, collectors, and privacy-conscious users active on X and Discord.

---

## Rewards

Users earn **Raven Points (RP)** via on-chain and social media Missions.

### On-Chain Missions

| Mission                         | RP  | Frequency |
| ------------------------------- | --- | --------- |
| Launch a Collection             | 50  | 1/week    |
| Mint an NFT Privately           | 15  | 8/week    |
| Mint NFT Publicly               | 10  | 8/week    |
| List an NFT on Marketplace      | 10  | 10/week   |
| Purchase Listed NFT             | 12  | 7/week    |

## How It Works

1. **Complete Missions**: Perform on-chain tasks (e.g., minting, trading) or social actions (e.g., retweeting, Discord engagement).
2. **Unlock Rewards**:
   - **NFTs**: Exclusive digital assets.
   - **Discounts**: Lower fees on marketplace or minting.
   - **Governance**: Voting in community decisions.
   - **Future Tokens**: RP influences community token allocation.
